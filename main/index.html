Q&A

Have questions? We have answers.

Question 1: How does yomofo delivery and curbside pickup work?
Answer 1: yomofo makes it easy to order from your favorite stores.
Shop for items from stores near you, with a variety of retailers and trusted local grocers across the country.
Then, yomofo will connect you with a personal shopper in your area to shop and deliver your order. Contactless delivery is available with our “Leave at my door” option.
You can track your order’s progress and communicate with your shopper every step of the way using the yomofo app or our website.
Yomofo also offers curbside pickup at select retail locations. Simply place your order and choose a pickup time, and a shopper will prepare your order at the store.
When you get to the store, use the yomofo app to notify us. Depending on the store, a shopper or store employee will bring the groceries to your car, or you can pick them up at the designated area.
Question 2: How much does yomofo cost?
Answer 2: You don’t need a membership to order with yomofo.
To get started, create an account, select the store you want to shop, and place your order. New customers may be eligible for free delivery promotions.
Delivery: Fees start at $1.99 for same-day orders over $35. Fees vary for one-hour deliveries, club store deliveries, and deliveries under $35.
Pickup: There may be a “pickup fee” (equivalent to a delivery fee for pickup orders) on your pick up order.
Service fees: Service fees vary and are subject to change based on factors like location and the number and types of items in your cart.
With an optional Yomofo+ membership, you can get $0 delivery fee on every order over $43 and lower service fees too.
Question 3: Will i pay the same price on yomofo as i would on store?
Answer 3: Retail partners set the prices of items on the yomofo marketplace. While many retailers offer everyday store prices on yomofo, some retailers may set prices on the yomofo platform that are different from in-store prices.
You can view pricing policies for each retailer on the yomofo app and website.
Question 4: What happens if something is out of stock and I need to give specific instructions?
Answer 4: YoMoFo makes it easy to communicate with your shopper. When an item you want is out-of-stock at the store, your shopper will follow your replacement preferences.
You can set item and delivery instructions in advance, as well as chat directly with your shopper while they shop and deliver your items. You can tell the shopper to:
Find Best Match: By default, your shopper will use their best judgement to pick a replacement for your item.
Pick Specific Replacement: You can pick a specific alternative for the shopper to purchase if your first choice is out-of-stock.
Don’t Replace: For items you’d rather not replace, choose “Don’t replace” to get a refund if the item is out of stock.
You can update or cancel your order as long as your shopper hasn’t started shopping. If you need to make changes after shopping has started, you can use the app or website to approve replacements or chat with your shopper.
Question 5: What happens if there's an issue with my order?
Answer 5: If something isn’t right, you’ve got options. In the YoMoFo app or website, you can report:
Missing items
Incorrect items
Damaged items
Poor replacements
Early/late orders
If an order never came, or you get someone else’s order, you can reach out to YoMoFo Customer Experience.
